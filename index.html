More about Me

My name is Melanie Jumalon, a Calgary-based Full Stack Developer with over a decade of healthcare experience, blending technical expertise with strong problem-solving and project management skills. I specialize in backend development, building scalable and efficient solutions tailored to meet diverse user needs.

My passion for technology began at 12 when I disassembled and rebuilt my first computer—a pivotal moment that sparked my love for tech. In healthcare, I witnessed how software often focuses narrowly on specific users, which inspired me to pursue development to create more inclusive, impactful solutions.

I thrive on collaborating with startups and established enterprises, embracing challenges, and bringing innovative ideas to life. Driven by a passion for problem-solving and optimization, I aim to build meaningful systems that leave a positive impact.
